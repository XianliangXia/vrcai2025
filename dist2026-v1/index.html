Conference Overview

In November 2026, the 21st ACM SIGGRAPH International Conference on Virtual Reality Continuum and its Applications (VRCAI2026) will take place just before SIGGRAPH Asia 2026 (17-20th, Dec) in Hangzhou, China.

Since its inception in Hangzhou, China in 1993, VRCAI has grown into a well-established international established conference with a strong emphasis on industrial relevance. The Virtual Reality Continuum (VRC) is expanding at an unprecedented pace, driven by substantial commercial investment and repaid technological innovations.

VRCAI aims to address the needs of the rapidly evolving field by fostering collaborations across the full continuum of next generation innovations, applications, and fundamental research. Encompassing Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Virtuality (AV), to Augmented Reality (AR), Mixed Reality (MR) Virtual Storytelling, Virtual Production, and Extended Reality (XR), the VRC represents the diverse ways in which digital technologies are transforming and integrating with the physical world.

Sponsored by ACM SIGGRAPH, VRCAI 2026 provides a premier international forum for researchers and practitioners from academia and industry to present and exchange their latest results, applications, experiments, experiences and evaluations.

Conference Theme

VRCAI 2026

With the rise of the metaverse concept and the rapid development of artificial intelligence technologies, the Virtual Reality Continuum is facing unprecedented opportunities and challenges. VRCAI 2026 will focus on the core theme of "Reality Fusion," exploring how to drive innovation and application of virtual reality technologies in the era of metaverse and artificial intelligence.

VRCAI2026 will focus on the following main themes:

  1. Fundamental and Related Technology in Computer Graphics
  2. Virtual Reality Continuum (VRC) Systems
  3. Three-dimensional Interaction and User Interfaces
  4. Virtual Storytelling, Virtual Production and Industry Applications

WorkShop

International Workshop on Web3D & Immersive Web (The Web3D Workshop at VRCAI 2026)

Web3D 2026 explores how immersive 3D environments delivered and shared through the Web are transforming the future of digital interaction, and how recent advances in AI capabilities, embodied intelligent systems, and multimodal human interfaces are expanding what is possible in XR.
