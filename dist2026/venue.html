Conference Venue

The ACM SIGGRAPH VRCAI 2026 conference will be held at the Hangzhou International Innovation Institute of Beihang University, Hangzhou, Zhejiang Province, China.

Hangzhou International Innovation Institute of Beihang University

The Hangzhou International Innovation Institute of Beihang University is established by Beihang University and the People's Government of Hangzhou Municipality jointly, to support Beihang University in carrying out high-level international education and other related work in Hangzhou.

Local Attractions

Hangzhou offers a unique blend of Chinese and Portuguese cultures, reflected in its architecture, cuisine, and traditions. We encourage attendees to explore the following attractions during their stay:

West Lake

A UNESCO World Heritage site famed for its timeless "Ten Scenes" like Spring Dawn on Su Causeway.

Liangzhu Ancient City

Archaeological proof of China's 5,000-year civilization, with ancient ruins and a museum.

Lingyin Temple

A serene, centuries-old Buddhist sanctuary nestled in a tranquil mountainous setting.
